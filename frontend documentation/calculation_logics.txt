==============================================================================
PLUS33 COFFEE ERP -- DASHBOARD WIDGET CALCULATION LOGIC & FORMULAS
==============================================================================

This document details the updated business logic, metrics, and formulas used
for the Sales Overview and Financial Overview (MTD) dashboard widgets.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. SALES OVERVIEW WIDGET (Sales Chart)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The Sales Overview widget presents a consolidated national view of sales
performance.

* METRIC: Total Sales
  - Description: The gross volume of sales aggregated from all active operating
                 regions (France, India, UAE).
  - Formula:
    Total Sales = Sum(Regional Sales of France, India, UAE)

* METRIC: Sales Target & Target Achievement
  - Description: Since this view represents the consolidated national income
                 without a defined baseline benchmark, there is no target
                 assigned to this national Sales Overview dashboard view.
  - Formula:
    Sales Target = N/A (Hidden from UI)
    Target Achievement % = N/A (Hidden from UI)

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. FINANCIAL OVERVIEW WIDGET (Financial Chart - MTD)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The Financial Overview widget displays month-to-date (MTD) summary metrics,
comparing gross income against established store targets.

* METRIC: Total Income
  - Description: The total gross revenue generated from completed coffee and
                 merchandise sales across all stores.
  - Formula:
    Total Income = Total Revenue (Gross Sales)

* METRIC: Total Target
  - Description: The consolidated sales target established for active stores. Each store
                 has a fixed baseline target of 50,000.00 EUR. The regional target is
                 the sum of all stores in the region, and the national target is the
                 sum of all regions' targets.
  - Formula:
    Total Target = Active Store Count * 50,000.00 EUR

* METRIC: Profit
  - Description: The financial net margin calculated directly relative to the
                 target.
  - Formula:
    Profit = Total Income - Total Target
  - Note: Profit is represented as a negative currency value when under-target.

* METRIC: Franchise Royalty
  - Description: The royalty fee calculated on gross income.
  - Formula:
    Franchise Royalty = Total Income * 0.05
