Financial Overview Analytics

Total Income

The total income generated from completed coffee and merchandise sales across all stores during this period (exclusive of taxes).

Total Target

The consolidated sales target established for active stores. Each store has a fixed target of 50,000 EUR. Region targets are the sum of all store targets, and the national target sums all region targets.

Profit

The financial net margin relative to targets, calculated as: Total Income - Total Target.

Franchise Royalty & Budget

Royalty: Calculated at 5% of gross revenue representing franchise fee overheads. Budget: Represents target budget allocations compared to actual cash reserves.
