Clock-In & Attendance Policy View shift allocation rules, location requirements & auto clock-out guidelines
📋
Shift Required to Clock In

You can only clock in on days where a shift has been assigned to you by your supervisor. On rest days, you will see a "Rest Day" notice — you may request overtime from that screen.

📍
Must Be Within 30 Metres of Store

Your device location is checked when you tap Clock In. You must be within 30 metres of the store entrance. If you are too far away, clock-in will be blocked and your exact distance will be shown.

🔄
Location Checked Every 5 Minutes While On Duty

Once clocked in, your location is verified every 5 minutes in the background. You will receive a warning notification if you move close to the store boundary.

🚪
Auto Clock-Out Beyond 200 Metres

If you move more than 200 metres from the store without an approved away pass, the system will automatically clock you out.

⏰
10-Minute Grace Period

When an approved away pass expires or you step outside the boundary, you have a 10-minute grace period to return before auto clock-out activates. You may also request a time extension from your supervisor during this window.

📶
Network Outage Protection

If your device loses internet connection, you will not be automatically clocked out. When connection is restored, your current location is checked before any action is taken.

✅
Away Pass

If you need to leave the store premises during your shift, you can request an Away Pass from your dashboard. Your supervisor approves it with a set time limit and you will be notified when it expires.
