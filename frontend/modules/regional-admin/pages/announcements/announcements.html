Broadcast Announcement Policy

⏳
15-Day Auto-Disappear Rule

Broadcast messages stay active in store user chat & feeds for 15 days from creation. After 15 days, they automatically disappear from active user views.

🗄️
60-Day Soft-Delete Retention

Deleted announcements remain soft-deleted in the database for 60 days for compliance before being permanently purged.

🎨
Publisher Role Colors & Badges

🏷️
Priority Tag Classification Guide
CRITICAL ALERT Emergency directive or urgent safety alert requiring immediate action.
COMPANY BULLETIN Enterprise policy update, SOP change, or corporate circular.
STANDARD NOTICE Daily store update, shift handover notes, or general reminder.
📎
Multi-Format Media Support

Broadcasts support direct inline rendering for Video (.mp4), Audio (.mp3), PDF, Office Documents (.docx, .xlsx, .zip), and Images.
